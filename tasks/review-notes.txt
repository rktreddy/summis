RESUBMISSION addressing prior rejection where in-app purchases completed but did not grant Pro access.

WHAT WAS FIXED
The app checked for an entitlement named "pro_access" but our RevenueCat configuration uses "Summis Pro". After a successful purchase the receipt was validated correctly, but the client-side check failed to recognize the entitlement, so the UI remained on the Free tier. The identifier is now aligned. Purchases flip the tier to Pro immediately and Restore Purchases works end-to-end.

5. Subscribe to Pro Monthly ($7.99) - verifies the purchase path works for the second product
