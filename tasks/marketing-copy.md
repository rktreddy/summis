## 1. App Store Description (iOS & Google Play)

Your phone is making you less focused — even when you're not using it.

The landmark "Brain Drain" study (Ward et al., 2017) proved that the mere presence of your smartphone reduces available cognitive capacity. You don't have to be scrolling. You don't have to hear a notification. Just having your phone nearby measurably impairs your ability to think.

Summis is a cognitive performance coach built on this research. It coaches you through science-backed focus practices, tracks whether you actually follow them, and then proves which ones work — with your own data.

**Focus Sprints**
Summis guides you through a structured 4-phase focus protocol developed from Dr. Sahar Yousef's research at UC Berkeley:

1. Set your intention (30 seconds)
2. Deep focus with phone away (30, 45, or 50 minutes)
3. Genuine rest — walk, stretch, look outside (5-10 minutes)
4. Reflect on focus quality (10 seconds)

No gamification gimmicks. No animations competing for your attention. Just a minimal timer and a system that works.

**Cognitive Hygiene Tracking**
Your environment shapes your cognition. Summis tracks the practices that matter — phone placement, notification management, workspace setup — and scores your daily compliance. Not another habit tracker. A focused system for the variables that actually affect your thinking.

**The Correlation Engine**
This is what sets Summis apart. After 30 days of data, Summis connects your cognitive hygiene practices to your actual focus quality. Instead of generic claims like "put your phone away to focus better," you see: "On the 22 days you kept your phone in another room, your focus rating was 31% higher."

Your data. Your correlations. Evidence you generated yourself.

**Most Important Tasks (MITs)**
Set up to 3 MITs each morning. Summis connects task completion to your sprint performance, so you can see which conditions help you get meaningful work done — not just stay busy.

**Chronotype-Aware Coaching**
Based on the Horne-Ostberg morningness-eveningness framework, Summis maps your personal energy pattern (Peak, Dip, Recovery) and coaches you to schedule deep work, administrative tasks, and creative thinking at the right times. Set your chronotype during onboarding and get a personalized daily energy blueprint.

**Your Cognitive Performance Score**
A single daily score built from sprint completion (30%), focus quality (30%), hygiene compliance (25%), and MIT completion (15%). Track 7-day and 30-day trends. See whether your systems are actually improving your output over time.

**Built to be used less, not more.**
Summis is designed for under 5 minutes of daily interaction. The goal is better focus in the real world, not more time in the app.

**How to test the core sprint flow:**
1. Open the app and complete onboarding (select chronotype, configure hygiene practices).
2. Navigate to the Today tab. Tap "Start Sprint."
3. The sprint has 4 phases: Intention (write what you'll work on) -> Focus (countdown timer) -> Rest (guided break) -> Reflection (rate focus quality 1-5). You can use a short 30-minute sprint for testing.
4. After completing a sprint, the Score tab shows your performance data.

**Low daily active usage is by design.**
Summis is a cognitive performance coach, not an engagement app. The app is designed for under 5 minutes of daily interaction. Users open it to start a focus sprint, set MITs, and check their score. Low session duration and low DAU are intentional product goals — success means users are focused on real work, not the app.
